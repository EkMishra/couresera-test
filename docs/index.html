Instruments

Guitar

The guitar is a popular string instrument with versatile sounds. It comes in various types, including acoustic, electric, and classical, and has been played by legendary guitarists in genres like rock, blues, jazz, and more. Its strings and playing techniques contribute to its unique appeal.

Piano

The piano is a versatile keyboard instrument with a wide range and expressive sound. It comes in grand and upright styles, played by notable pianists in classical, jazz, and popular genres. Pedals enhance the sound, and its influence spans music education and performance worldwide.

Violin

The violin is a versatile string instrument with origins in Italy. It produces expressive sound through bowing or plucking. Notable violinists include Vivaldi and Perlman. It is cherished for its rich musicality and is widely used in various genres.
